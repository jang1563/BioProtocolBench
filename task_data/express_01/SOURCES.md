## Included Sources

### Silver

1. Rosano GL, Ceccarelli EA. *Recombinant protein expression in Escherichia coli: advances and challenges.* Frontiers in Microbiology. 2014. DOI: https://doi.org/10.3389/fmicb.2014.00172
   - Used for: BL21(DE3) and derivatives as canonical T7 expression hosts, 0.1-1 mM IPTG induction range, mid-log induction OD600 0.4-0.8, standard induction temperatures (18 / 25 / 30 / 37 C), low-temperature induction for fusion solubility, typical MBP-fusion yields in the tens of mg/L range.

### Bronze

1. QIAGEN. *QIAexpressionist Handbook (5th ed.).* https://www.qiagen.com/us/resources/resourcedetail?id=79ca2f7d-42fe-4d62-8676-4cfa948c9435
   - Used for: Ni-NTA-compatible lysis buffer pH 7.0-8.0 window.

2. NEB. *pMAL Protein Fusion and Purification System.* https://www.neb.com/en-us/products/e8200-pmal-protein-fusion-and-purification-system
   - Used for: MBP-GFP fusion target identification and approximate molecular weight (~72 kDa).

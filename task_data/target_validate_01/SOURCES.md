## Included Sources

### Silver

1. Koscielny et al. *Open Targets: a platform for therapeutic target identification and validation*.
   - DOI: 10.1093/nar/gkw1055
   - Used to justify target-aware evidence review before experiment selection.

2. Blake. *Target validation in drug discovery*.
   - DOI: 10.1385/1-59745-217-3:367
   - Used to justify choosing an informative first validation experiment.

3. Plenge, Scolnick, and Altshuler. *Validating therapeutic targets through human genetics*.
   - DOI: 10.1038/nrd4051
   - Used to justify focused validation against a therapeutic hypothesis before broader branching.

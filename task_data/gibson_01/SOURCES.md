## Included Sources

### Silver

1. Gibson DG, Young L, Chuang RY, Venter JC, Hutchison CA, Smith HO. *Enzymatic assembly of DNA molecules up to several hundred kilobases.* Nature Methods. 2009. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1038/nmeth.1318
   - Used for: the canonical isothermal Gibson master-mix formulation (T5 exonuclease + Phusion polymerase + Taq ligase), 50 C optimum, 20-40 bp homology overlap range, and >80% correct-assembly baseline for 2-3 fragment reactions.

### Bronze

1. NEB. *NEBuilder HiFi DNA Assembly Master Mix Protocol (E2621 / E5510).* https://www.neb.com/en-us/protocols/2014/11/26/gibson-assembly-protocol-e5510
   - Used for: 15 min at 50 C recommendation for 2-3 fragment assemblies.

2. NEB. *NEB 5-alpha Competent E. coli.* https://www.neb.com/en-us/products/c2987-neb-5-alpha-competent-e-coli-high-efficiency
   - Used for: 100 ug/mL ampicillin selection.

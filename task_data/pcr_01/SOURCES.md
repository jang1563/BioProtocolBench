## Included Sources

### Silver

1. Henke W, Herdel K, Jung K, Schnorr D, Loening SA. *Betaine improves the PCR amplification of GC-rich DNA sequences.* Nucleic Acids Research. 1997. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/25.19.3957
   - Used for: supporting the need for GC-rich PCR additives.

2. Chakrabarti R, Schutt CE. *The enhancement of PCR amplification by low molecular-weight sulfones.* Nucleic Acids Research. 2001. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/29.11.2377
   - Used for: supporting additive-assisted amplification of difficult templates.

3. Cline J, Braman JC, Hogrefe HH. *PCR fidelity of pfu DNA polymerase and other thermostable DNA polymerases.* Nucleic Acids Research. 1996. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/24.18.3546
   - Used for: supporting the preference for proofreading/high-fidelity polymerases over Taq.

2. Thermo Fisher. *Phusion High-Fidelity DNA Polymerase.* https://www.thermofisher.com/order/catalog/product/F530S

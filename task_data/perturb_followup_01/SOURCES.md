## Included Sources

### Silver

1. Koscielny et al. *Open Targets: a platform for therapeutic target identification and validation*.
   - DOI: 10.1093/nar/gkw1055
   - Used to justify evidence-backed target-profile inspection before prioritization or follow-up.

2. Dixit et al. *Perturb-Seq: Dissecting Molecular Circuits with Scalable Single-Cell RNA Profiling of Pooled Genetic Screens*.
   - DOI: 10.1016/j.cell.2016.11.038
   - Used to justify a perturbation-centric, orthogonal-follow-up decision surface.

3. Blake. *Target validation in drug discovery*.
   - DOI: 10.1385/1-59745-217-3:367
   - Used to justify focused validation of one candidate before broader experimental branching.

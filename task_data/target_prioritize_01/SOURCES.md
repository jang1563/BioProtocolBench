## Included Sources

### Silver

1. Plenge, Scolnick, and Altshuler. *Validating therapeutic targets through human genetics*.
   - DOI: 10.1038/nrd4051
   - Used to justify prioritizing target biology by translational and patient-relevant evidence.

2. Ochoa et al. *Open Targets Platform: supporting systematic drug-target identification and prioritisation*.
   - DOI: 10.1093/nar/gkaa1027
   - Used to justify context-aware synthesis across multiple target-disease evidence sources.

3. Carss et al. *Using human genetics to improve safety assessment of therapeutics*.
   - DOI: 10.1038/s41573-022-00561-w
   - Used to justify liability-aware target triage.

4. Dixit et al. *Perturb-Seq: Dissecting Molecular Circuits with Scalable Single-Cell RNA Profiling of Pooled Genetic Screens*.
   - DOI: 10.1016/j.cell.2016.11.038
   - Used to justify perturbation-driven candidate ranking and follow-up.
